MIAMI

FUN FACTS

  1. Named 'the 305' because of the local area code and 'Magic City'
  2. A nickname associated with Miami is the “Cruise Capital of the World” because of the Port of Miami, which is the largest cruise ship port in the world.
  3. Second most visited city in the US by foreign tourists in 2020.
> Miami is a city located in Miami-Dade County Florida. It is one of the most well-known cities in Florida, and the second-largest city in the state and a major hub for international trade, finance, culture, and the arts. Its cultural connections to Latin America has earned it the nickname, “Gateway to the Americas.”
