STATE OF FLORIDA

Florida is known for its amazing beaches, mild-to-hot climate, and flourishing tourism industry. It is the only state to border both the Atlantic Ocean and the Gulf of Mexico as it is located in the southeastern corner of the United States. Florida's capital is Tallahassee, and it's most populous city is Jacksonville; it also contains 59 cities with a population of over 50,000. This website will show data about three of Florida's cities: Tallahassee, Miami, and Pensacola. For more information about Florida visit: Florida Cities by Population (2024)
